About Nthawi Farms

Services offered by nthawi farms range from training, selling best organic farm produce for a better Malawi

The Management

Philosophy

Nthawi Farms is an establishment dedicated to sustainable organic farming and agricultural education. We provide high-quality farm produce and comprehensive training programs to support a healthier and more self-sufficient Malawi.

Our farm operations include crop cultivation, livestock management, and soil conservation techniques. We offer modern farming equipment, quality seeds, organic fertilizers, and storage facilities. Smaller-scale farmers benefit greatly from our accessible resources and expert guidance.

Larger agricultural ventures can access our advanced facilities including irrigation systems, processing centers, agricultural research services, equipment rental, training workshops, community outreach programs, and market linkage services to ensure sustainable growth and profitability.
